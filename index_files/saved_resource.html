F A Q s

Got questions? We've got answers! Explore our FAQs to learn all about our meme coin adventure." 🚀💡

1. What is Snoopy AI staking and how does it work?

Snoopy AI staking allows you to lock Snoopy AI tokens in our smart contract to earn rewards based on your chosen plan (30, 60, or 90 days). Each plan offers a different APY, and rewards are calculated based on the lock period and deposited amount.

2. What are the staking plans available?

There are three staking plans: 30 days with 1080% APY, 60 days with 1800% APY, and 90 days with 2880% APY. You can choose a plan when depositing, and tokens are locked until the period ends.

3. Are there any fees or minimum requirements for staking?

A 1% fee is charged on deposits, sent to the developer wallet. The minimum deposit is 1 Snoopy AI token. To earn leaderboard points, a minimum deposit of 120 tokens is required.

4. How can I earn referral rewards?

You can earn a 9% referral bonus by inviting others to stake using your referral address. Additionally, deposits of 10 or more tokens by your referrals grant you 6 leaderboard points.

5. How do I contact support for staking issues?

For any staking-related questions or issues, contact our team through our Telegram. We’re available to assist you!
